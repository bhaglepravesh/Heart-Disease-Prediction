Understanding Heart Disease

Heart disease is a leading cause of death worldwide. It's important to be aware of the risk factors and how to maintain a healthy heart.

Risk Factors

  • High blood pressure
  • High cholesterol
  • Diabetes
  • Smoking
  • Family history of heart disease
  • Unhealthy diet
  • Lack of physical activity

Prevention and Treatment

There are many things you can do to reduce your risk of heart disease. These include:

  • Eating a healthy diet low in saturated fat and cholesterol
  • Maintaining a healthy weight
  • Exercising regularly
  • Managing stress
  • Following your doctor's recommendations for medication
